*Creative Ideas for*
*Traveling Designers.*

*by Jolie Kingston*
*Principle Product Designer, LivingType*

*As a remote designer, it can be a challenge to collaborate with your team. Even though technology makes up for it 90% of the way, there's still that 10%. Body language, tone, water-cooler conversations. You miss the in-between moments that help you understand what someone is looking for and why. Fortunately, I've learned a handful of strategies to bridge the gap and get the inside scoop so you can do your best work no matter where you're working.*
